Classification with Decision trees

A Decision Tree is a Machine Learning algorithm that is used mainly for classification problems. It can be applied to categorical and continuous data. The main concept of the algorithm is simple; the data are split consecutively according to certain splitting criteria.

Decision trees are computationally efficient when training and quite fast for classifying new instances. Furthermore, they are comprehensional when they are not too large, therefore they are frequently used for data exploration. A common pitfall is that they are sensitive to overfitting, which can be confronted by pruning or by adding further criteria when building the tree.

             data=trainingdata,
